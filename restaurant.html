Our Specialities:

Natukodi Palav is a traditional chicken pulao from the Andhra Pradesh and Telangana regions in India. The term "natukodi" refers to country chicken or free-range chicken, known for its richer flavor and firmer texture compared to broiler chicken. This dish is a hearty and aromatic rice preparation, combining the robust taste of natukodi with fragrant basmati rice and an array of spices.Ragi Sangati (or Ragi Mudde) is a wholesome and nutritious dish made from finger millet flour, a staple in many parts of South India, especially Andhra Pradesh and Karnataka.Nellore Chepala Pulusu is a spicy and tangy fish curry originating from the Nellore district in Andhra Pradesh.
